Our tutors cover a wide range of subjects

Maths

Mathematics is a subject that is essential for students of all ages and disciplines. This course is designed to provide students with a solid foundation in mathematical concepts and skills, including algebra, geometry, trigonometry, and calculus. By the end of the course, students will be able to: ▪ Solve mathematical problems using algebra, geometry, and trigonometry ▪ Understand the basics of calculus and its applications ▪ Apply mathematical concepts to real-world problems ▪ Develop critical thinking and problem-solving skills

Arts/English

English is a global language and essential for communication in today's world. This course is designed to provide students with a solid foundation in English language skills, including grammar, reading comprehension, writing, and speaking. By the end of the course, students will be able to: ▪ Understand and apply key principles of English grammar ▪ Develop reading comprehension skills for various types of texts ▪ Write effectively in English for various purposes ▪ Speak English with clarity and confidence

Arabic

Learning Arabic and Quranic studies are essential for practicing Muslims and anyone interested in gaining a deeper understanding of the religion and culture. This course is designed to provide students with a solid foundation in Arabic language and Quranic studies. By the end of the course, students will be able to: ▪ Read and write in Arabic script ▪ Develop a basic understanding of Arabic grammar ▪ Understand the meaning and interpretation of key Quranic verses ▪ Develop a basic vocabulary of Quranic Arabic ▪ Understand the role of the Quran in Islamic tradition

Introduction to Web Development/Javascrit

In today's digital age, web development is a highly sought-after skill that has become essential for businesses and organizations of all types. This course is designed to provide students with a foundational understanding of web development concepts, including HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. By the end of the course, students will be able to: ▪ Develop a basic website using HTML and CSS ▪ Create interactive web pages using JavaScript ▪ Understand the basics of responsive design ▪ Troubleshoot common web development issues
